Nations Reach Network is dedicated to bring the good news of Jesus Christ through Media, Mercy and Missions. Previously, Nations Reach was known as Into Action Ministries in India, where we focused on producing Christian media materials and evangelistic videos and television programs. Spreading Gospel in to the Nations through Media is our primary focus. Mercy and other Mission activities come along to fulfill this great commission.

Into Action Media : A Media Division of Nations Reach. Proclaiming the gospel using every aspect of media. Producing and broadcasting in Indian vernacular languages. Our motto is " To bring hope to the people of the world".

Little Roses Home : A Mercy Division of Nations Reach A Mercy Division of Nations Reach Helping orphaned children, providing education to the poorest of the poor, Establishing training centers to equip the needy ones.

International Christian Fellowship : A Mission Division of Nations Reach A Mission Division of Nations Reach Helping indigenous workers with leadership seminars, Equipping them with resources to reach their targeted area and church planting in Indian subcontinent.
